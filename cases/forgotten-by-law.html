Forgotten by Law

An investigative short film documenting rural land pollution and legal oversights affecting marginalized agricultural communities.

Director & Producer
AAHSFF 2025 (NYC AMC Theatres)
Environmental Justice & Rural Law
Short Film / Documentary

Overview

Produced to expose the systemic gap between environmental regulation on paper and enforcement on the ground. The film spotlights agricultural communities affected by unregulated industrial dumping in rural sectors.

Impact & Festival Screening

Selected for official screening at the All American High School Film Festival (AAHSFF) 2025 at AMC Theatres in Times Square, NYC. Demonstrates narrative storytelling, field reporting, and visual direction capabilities alongside quantitative technical skills.
